Is EdTech solving the right problem in India?

By Anmol Mathur | Published on October 25, 2025

We're flooded with news about AI-driven learning and virtual reality classrooms. While innovation is exciting, it often overlooks a stark reality: the challenges of the digital divide and affordability that persist across the country indiabusiness.com . Access to advanced technology and the internet remains unequal, especially in rural areas, and the high cost of EdTech tools can be a significant barrier for many families indiabusiness.com .
